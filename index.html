Inside Sales Manager (ISM)

Turn Conversations into Commissions.

Inside Sales Manager (ISM) connects companies with motivated sales reps through real opportunities that support referral fees and commissions.

For Sales Reps

  • Browse and request real sales opportunities
  • Message companies inside the app
  • Track opportunity status
  • Earn referral fees or commissions

For Companies

  • Post sales opportunities
  • Engage interested reps
  • Control terms and visibility
  • Expand coverage without hiring full-time staff
